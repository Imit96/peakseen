## Overview

A fintech startup needed a brand identity that communicated trust, innovation, and accessibility. Their existing brand felt generic and failed to differentiate them in a crowded market.

## The Challenge

The existing visual identity relied heavily on blue — the default colour for every fintech company. There was no brand voice guide, inconsistent social media presence, and a logo that could belong to any company in any industry.

## The Approach

We started with a brand strategy workshop to define their core positioning: making financial tools accessible to first-time investors. This insight drove every design decision that followed.

The colour palette shifted from generic corporate blue to a warmer, more approachable combination that maintained professional credibility while feeling welcoming.

## The Work

The deliverables included a complete logo system, colour palette, typography selection, brand voice guidelines, and social media templates. Everything was documented in a comprehensive brand guidelines PDF.

## The Outcome

The rebrand launched across all touchpoints simultaneously. Within three months, the client reported a 40% increase in user trust scores from their quarterly survey and a 25% increase in sign-up conversion rates.
